Instructions

The closer your assumptions about how much others will keep for themselves, the more you will earn.

If your guess is exactly the same as the average amount that other participants have left for themselves, you will get 20 points.

Every other point will reduce your payout by 1 point.

If the difference between the real average amount stored by others and your assumption is more than 20 tokens, you will not get anything at this stage.
