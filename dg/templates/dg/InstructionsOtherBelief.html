Instructions

The closer will be your guess about how much others think a person should keep for him/herself the higher will be your earnings.

If your guess will be exactly the same as an average amount the other participants believe a person should keep, you will earn 20 points.

Every point of different will decrease your payoff by 1 point.

If the difference between is more than 20 tokens you will receive nothing from this stage.
